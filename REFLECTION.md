# Reflection

During this assignment, one of the main challenges was balancing the needs of different stakeholders.

For example, students require a fast and easy-to-use system, while IT staff prioritize system stability and security. This created a trade-off between usability and performance.

Another challenge was ensuring that all requirements were clear and measurable. Writing vague requirements can lead to misunderstandings during development.

To address this, all requirements were written with acceptance criteria to ensure clarity and traceability.

This process helped improve my understanding of requirements engineering and the importance of stakeholder analysis in building successful systems.
