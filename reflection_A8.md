# Reflection

One of the main challenges in this assignment was determining the appropriate level of detail for state and activity diagrams. Too much detail made diagrams difficult to read, while too little detail reduced their usefulness.

Another challenge was aligning diagrams with Agile user stories and functional requirements from previous assignments. Ensuring consistency across all artifacts required careful mapping.

State diagrams focus on how objects behave and change over time, while activity diagrams focus on workflows and processes. Understanding this distinction was important in modeling the system correctly.

Overall, this assignment improved my ability to model system behavior and workflows, which is essential for software design and implementation.
